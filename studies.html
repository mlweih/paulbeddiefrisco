Case Studies

Sustainability and Management consultant Engagements that FutureMax as has delivered.

A Collection of Work

FutureMax believes that sustainability, when maximised through nature, people and technology, achieves great outcomes and makes a significant impact. The first step of this is building great working relationships. We love seeing the changes that can be created and the results that bring a smile to a CEO and their team when a vision is realised. A couple of projects we have worked on are:

A Diversified US Technology and Manufacturing company

The client was under government pressure to eliminate fugitive VOC emissions from chemical process equipment in a very short time. The solution we gave was a custom set of interventions including VOC monitoring, physical barriers and reconfigured exhaust ducting. The result was an average 93% reduction in fugitive VOCs achieved in less than three months along with other results.

German/Japanese Healthcare Leader

The client wanted a holistic and integrated building improvement and wellness program for its Asia HQ. It was decided to undertake a significant upgrade of the facility including the creation of the primary Asia training facilities for the client’s end customers. The solution was to create a holistic and integrated building improvement program, using smart building and sustainability technology, to improve the client’s facility across a wide range of measurable areas. The cost savings from the energy and facility management initiatives subsidized the indoor environment and IT initiatives and provided an attractive payback for the whole program along with many other great results.

Large Private Equity backed Real Estate Investment Fund in Asia

This client wanted automation of GRESB reporting, Analytics and collection of monthly utility data from multiple real estate assets located in cities across Asia. FutureMax recommend our Partner, GOBY, to provide this client with their world class SaaS solution for the annual GRESB data gathering and reporting and analysis tools. The client is expected to reduce the energy consumption of the assets included in the program by up to 4% simply through the automation and analytics of the utility data by identifying opportunities to improve building energy performance and improving their overall GRESB score in a managed and intentional manner.
